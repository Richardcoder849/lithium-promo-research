## 通用搜索组合公式

```
{国家关键词} + {平台类型} + {battery/lithium/energy storage} + {equipment/machinery} + {2025/2026}
```

示例：
- `Korea exhibition battery equipment 2025`
- `USA B2B platform lithium battery machinery`
- `Europe media energy storage industry news`
